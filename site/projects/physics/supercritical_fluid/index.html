Supercritical Fluid

After watching Cody from the YouTube channel Cody's Lab make supercritical CO2 in a glass tube, I was excited to replicate his experiment. I am fascinated by supercritical fluids and I thought it was cool that this experiment would be very inexpensive.

This experiment required five things

  • dry ice
  • small diameter borosilicate glass tube
  • propane torch
  • a way to weigh dry ice with about 0.02 gram accuracy
  • a chamber that could contain exploding glass tubes and has a window for filming
