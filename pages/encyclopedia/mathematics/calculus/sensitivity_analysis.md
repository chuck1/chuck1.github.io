# sensitivity analysis

## root sum of squares

In general

$$
e_y^2 = \sum \left( \frac{\partial y}{\partial x} e_x \right)^2
$$

### addition

$$
z = x + y
$$

$$
e_z^2 = \left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} e_x \right)^2 + \left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} e_y \right)^2
$$

$$
e_z^2 = e_x^2 + e_y^2
$$

### multiplication

$$
z = x y
$$

$$
e_z^2 = \left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} e_x \right)^2 + \left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} e_y \right)^2
$$

$$
e_z^2 = y^2 e_x^2 + x^2 e_y^2
$$
