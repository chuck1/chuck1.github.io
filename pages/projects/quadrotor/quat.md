
## Time Derivative

$$
\dot{q} = \omega q
$$

$$
\dot{q}^* = q^* \omega^*
$$

where

$$
\omega = \frac{1}{2}
\begin{bmatrix}
0 \\
\omega_x \\
\omega_y \\
\omega_z
\end{bmatrix}
$$

# Matrix Representaion}

Define

$$
A = \begin{bmatrix}
 a &  b &  c &  d \\
-b &  a & -d &  c \\
-c &  d &  a & -b \\
-d & -c &  b &  a
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
B = \begin{bmatrix}
 a & -b & -c & -d \\
 b &  a & -d &  c \\
 c &  d &  a & -b \\
 d & -c &  b &  a
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Multiplication

$$
ab = c
$$
$$
B(a) [b] = [c]
